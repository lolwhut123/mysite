Mechanics

AirBlade

Airblade is a technique to pull off when the enemy is Knocked Airborne and you are able to squeeze-in an extra Q damage off. This is done by Q<Airborning the Target>, and E-Q-R.

BeyBlade

Beyblade is simply done by e-qing (creating a circle) and flashing in the direction you desire. This gives the opponents no time to react to your move especially when you have a tornado.

KeyBlade

KeyBlade is a combination of the two previous Yasuo mechanics. In order to pull of a Keyblade, you must have
  1. A decent attack speed enough to make Yasuo's minimum Q cooldown as 1.33
  2. 2 stacks of Steel Tempest, giving you a tornado opportunity for the next 6 seconds

Windwall Breath

When you know for sure there is a Knocked-Up Target and you don't have the vision of them, you can immediately press W to create both windwall and vision of a brush/visionless spot, in order to ult the Knocked Up opponents. This vision gives more sight than you would normally anticipate.
