Many high-elo Yasuo mains are known to dodge every single skillshot in skirmishes and pull out such a beautiful outplay with the multi-people ult, as you see in montages.
The secret related to this is, that it is not necessary at all.

Yasuo can both be the initiator or the followup, but typically is used as the followup.
not the followup for hoping your teammate to knock up 5 enemies for you, no. How he approaches the fight is important. For example, If a fairly well-farmed Yasuo approaches the fight from the enemies' backline, it is highly likely that the fight is going to be a win.
>

The one fact you should keep in mind all the time, is that the reason why Yasuo is considered a difficult champion is because he has such a versatile kit.
meaning that in some games, you might wanna go for a risky play. In some games, you should build triforce into tank items. In some games, you should get a Frozen Mallet and use yourself as a ADC peel tool.

There are two most important things to keep in mind when playing Yasuo in teamfights. His wind wall utility and his sweeping blade (E).
Sweeping blade, there are more to talk about in Mechanics and Tricks session, but Wind walling the enemies' abilities can completely turn the fight around.
